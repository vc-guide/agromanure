Agri Asia 2025: This 14th International Exhibition on Agriculture Technologies will take place from September 18-20, 2025, at the Helipad Exhibition Centre in Gandhinagar, Gujarat, India. IFA Annual Conference 2025: Scheduled for May 12-14, 2025, this conference focuses on "Investing in the future of food" and will discuss sustainable practices and technological advancements in the fertilizer industry.
